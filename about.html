Our Mission

At SIIF, we empower students to become stewards of both capital and conscience. Our mission is to deliver real-world investing experience to university students while redefining the role finance can play in addressing social issues. We aim to build the next generation of investors who think critically, act ethically, and believe in the power of finance for good.
