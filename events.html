Our Flagship Events

Discover SIIF's premier events that connect students with industry professionals and provide valuable insights into the world of investments and finance.

Corporate Cocktails

Our signature networking event brings together industry professionals and students in a relaxed setting. Corporate Cocktails offers a unique opportunity to build connections with representatives from leading investment firms, banks, and financial institutions while enjoying refreshments and engaging conversations. This semi-annual event is perfect for students looking to expand their professional network and gain insights into the financial industry directly from those working in it.

Investing 101

Perfect for beginners, our Investing 101 workshop covers the fundamentals of investing in an approachable, easy-to-understand format. Learn about different asset classes, risk management, portfolio construction, and basic investment strategies. This interactive session is designed to build a solid foundation of knowledge for those new to investing, with plenty of opportunities to ask questions and engage with experienced investors from our team. Whether you're considering making your first investment or simply curious about the world of finance, this workshop will equip you with essential knowledge to start your investment journey.

DCF Workshop

Our Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Workshop provides an in-depth exploration of one of the most important valuation methodologies used by investment professionals. During this hands-on session, participants learn how to build a DCF model from scratch, forecast future cash flows, determine appropriate discount rates, and calculate terminal values. Led by experienced finance professionals and supported by our analysis team, this workshop combines theoretical knowledge with practical application. Participants will work through real company examples, gaining valuable skills that are directly applicable to careers in equity research, investment banking, and corporate finance.

Careers in Finance

Our Careers in Finance panel brings together professionals from various sectors of the financial industry to share their experiences, career paths, and insights. Featuring representatives from investment banking, asset management, private equity, fintech, and corporate finance, this event offers a comprehensive overview of the diverse opportunities within finance. Panelists discuss their day-to-day responsibilities, industry trends, recruitment processes, and offer advice on breaking into their respective fields. This event is particularly valuable for students considering their career options, offering candid perspectives on what it's really like to work in different areas of finance.

Stock Pitch Night

Stock Pitch Night is our premier competitive event where students showcase their analytical skills by presenting investment recommendations to a panel of industry judges. Participants research a company, develop a comprehensive investment thesis, and deliver a compelling presentation arguing why the stock is a buy, sell, or hold. This event simulates the real-world process used by investment analysts and fund managers, providing valuable experience in equity research, financial modeling, and presentation skills. Beyond the competition aspect, Stock Pitch Night creates a collaborative learning environment where participants receive constructive feedback from experienced professionals and peers. Winners receive recognition and sometimes prizes, but all participants gain invaluable experience that stands out on resumes.
